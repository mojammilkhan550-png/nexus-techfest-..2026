// 01 Signal

A festival shaped like a network

NEXUS isn't a string of disconnected events — it's a single graph of makers, hackers, roboticists, and designers, all routed through one weekend at IIT Bombay. Every node you touch — a workshop, a pitch, a 3AM debugging session — feeds the next.

Now in its seventh year, NEXUS draws teams from across the country to build, break, and ship in public, judged by engineers who've actually shipped things themselves.
